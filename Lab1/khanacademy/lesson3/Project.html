Recipe #1: Omelet

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 1
IngredientsQuantity
Egg2
Salt1 pinch
Oil1 tsp

Steps

  1. Beat eggs + salt.
  2. Heat pan with oil.
  3. Cook 2–3 minutes.
